## What I Do

I build and operate the infrastructure that turns LLMs into dependable, production-grade services. My focus is the full lifecycle: **high-throughput model serving**, **retrieval-augmented generation**, **automated evaluation**, and the **MLOps / CI-CD pipelines** that keep everything reproducible, observable, and quality-controlled.

**LLM Serving & Inference**  
Deploying and tuning LLMs in production with **vLLM** or **SGLang** continuous batching, tensor parallelism, KV-cache and quantization tricks for low-latency, high-throughput inference.

**Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**  
End-to-end RAG pipelines - ingestion, chunking, embeddings, vector retrieval, reranking, and grounding for accurate, context-aware answers backed by real sources.

**Automated Benchmarking & Evaluation**  
Repeatable eval harnesses that measure latency, throughput, and answer quality, so every model, prompt, or config change is validated with data instead of guesswork.

**Quality Control & Guardrails**  
Output validation, regression suites, and guardrails that keep LLM systems consistent, safe, and trustworthy as they evolve.

**MLOps & CI/CD**
Reproducible ML workflows with **MLflow** for experiment tracking and model registry, wired into automated CI/CD for testing, packaging, and deployment.

**Observability**  
Operational insight into live LLM services with the **Elastic / Kibana** stack for logs, metrics, and dashboards for performance, cost, and reliability.
